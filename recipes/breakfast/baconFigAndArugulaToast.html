Bacon, Fig, and Arugula Toast

Ingredients

  1. 1 medium Fig
  2. 1 strip of Bacon
  3. 1 oz of Mozzarella Cheese
  4. 1 cup of Arugula
  5. 1 Tbsp of Mayo like dressing
  6. 1 Slice of Multi grain bread

Directions

  1. Cook bacon in a pan over medium-high heat to desired crispiness. Slice figs and toast bread. Spread mayo on toasted bread and top with mozzarella, bacon, arugula, and figs. Serve and enjoy!

Nutrition facts

  • Calories 270
  • Carbs 25g
  • Fat 14g
  • Protein 13g
